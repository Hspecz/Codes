// Adding Custom Race (Riggo)
// 
//=====================
1. Open map.hpp
	Find enum e_race : int8{
	Before RC_MAX insert your custom race
2. Open script_constants.hpp
	Find export_constant(RC_ALL);
	Before that line put export_constant(RC_customrace);
3. Open clif.cpp
	Find case RC_FORMLESS:
	After the last RC entry put your custom race
4. Open battle.cpp
	Put your innate weakness and advantage of those custom races
5. Open atcommand.cpp
	Find ACMD_FUNC(mobinfo)
	Then put the race name
